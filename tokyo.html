7 Things to Do in Tokyo

1. Experience Shibuya Crossing

Shibuya Crossing is the busiest intersection in the world, offering a unique experience of Tokyo's bustling city life.

2. Visit Sensoji Temple

Sensoji Temple is Tokyo's oldest temple and a significant cultural landmark.

3. Climb Tokyo Skytree

Tokyo Skytree is the tallest structure in Japan, offering breathtaking views of the city.

4. Explore teamLab Borderless

teamLab Borderless is an immersive digital art museum, offering an unparalleled visual experience.

5. Visit the Imperial Palace

The Imperial Palace is the primary residence of the Emperor of Japan and offers beautiful gardens and historic architecture.

6. Shop in Ginza

Ginza is Tokyo's premier shopping district, offering luxury stores, dining, and entertainment.

7. Visit Tsukiji Fish Market

Tsukiji Fish Market is famous for its fresh seafood and bustling atmosphere.
