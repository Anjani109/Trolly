7 Things to Do in Barcelona

1. Visit the Sagrada Família

The Sagrada Família is an iconic basilica designed by Antoni Gaudí, a must-visit for its stunning architecture.

2. Explore Park Güell

Park Güell is another of Gaudí's masterpieces, featuring whimsical structures and beautiful gardens.

3. Stroll Down La Rambla

La Rambla is a vibrant street in the heart of Barcelona, perfect for a leisurely stroll among shops and street performers.

4. Wander the Gothic Quarter

The Gothic Quarter is full of narrow medieval streets, hidden squares, and historical landmarks.

5. Relax at Barceloneta Beach

Barceloneta Beach is the perfect place to unwind and enjoy the Mediterranean sun and sea.

6. Tour Camp Nou

Camp Nou is the home stadium of FC Barcelona and a must-visit for football fans.

7. Visit Montjuïc

Montjuïc offers stunning views of the city and is home to several attractions, including the Magic Fountain and Montjuïc Castle.
