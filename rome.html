7 Things to Do in Rome

1. Visit the Colosseum

The Colosseum is one of Rome's most iconic landmarks, offering a glimpse into ancient Roman history.

2. Explore the Vatican Museums

The Vatican Museums house an extensive collection of art and historical artifacts, including the Sistine Chapel.

3. Visit the Pantheon

The Pantheon is a marvel of ancient engineering, known for its massive dome and oculus.

4. Throw a Coin in the Trevi Fountain

The Trevi Fountain is a beautiful baroque fountain, famous for the tradition of throwing a coin over your shoulder to ensure a return to Rome.

5. Climb the Spanish Steps

The Spanish Steps are a popular meeting spot and offer a great place to relax and enjoy the view.

6. Explore the Roman Forum

The Roman Forum is a sprawling ruin of ancient government buildings and temples, offering a fascinating glimpse into Roman history.

7. Visit Castel Sant'Angelo

Castel Sant'Angelo is a historic fortress with stunning views of the Tiber River and Vatican City.
