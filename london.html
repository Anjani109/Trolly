7 Things to Do in London

1. Visit Big Ben

Big Ben is an iconic symbol of London, located at the north end of the Palace of Westminster.

2. Walk Across Tower Bridge

Tower Bridge is a stunning feat of Victorian engineering, offering panoramic views of London.

3. Explore the British Museum

The British Museum houses a vast collection of world art and artifacts, offering a journey through human history.

4. Ride the London Eye

The London Eye offers a unique perspective of the city, with stunning views from its observation pods.

5. Visit the Tower of London

The Tower of London is a historic castle and former royal residence, home to the Crown Jewels.

6. Visit Buckingham Palace

Buckingham Palace is the official residence of the British monarch, offering a glimpse into royal life.

7. Relax in Hyde Park

Hyde Park is one of London's largest parks, offering beautiful landscapes, recreational activities, and cultural events.
