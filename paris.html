7 Things to Do in Paris

1. Visit the Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world. Be sure to go to the top for an incredible view of Paris.

2. Explore the Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is home to thousands of works of art, including the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.

3. Stroll Down the Champs-Élysées

The Champs-Élysées is a beautiful avenue lined with shops, cafes, and theaters. It's a great place for a leisurely stroll.

4. Visit Notre-Dame Cathedral

Notre-Dame Cathedral is a stunning example of French Gothic architecture. Don't miss the chance to see it up close.

5. Take a Seine River Cruise

A Seine River cruise is a relaxing way to see the city and its landmarks from a different perspective.

6. Explore Montmartre

Montmartre is a historic district known for its artistic history and the stunning Sacré-Cœur Basilica.

7. Visit the Palace of Versailles

The Palace of Versailles is a short trip from Paris and offers a glimpse into the opulent lives of French royalty.
