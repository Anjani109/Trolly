7 Things to Do in Dubai

1. Visit Burj Khalifa

Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world, offering spectacular views from its observation decks.

2. Shop at Dubai Mall

Dubai Mall is one of the largest shopping malls in the world, offering a wide range of shops, restaurants, and attractions.

3. Watch the Dubai Fountain Show

The Dubai Fountain offers a stunning water and light show set to music, located at the base of Burj Khalifa.

4. Explore Palm Jumeirah

Palm Jumeirah is an artificial archipelago featuring luxury hotels, resorts, and stunning beaches.

5. Visit Burj Al Arab

Burj Al Arab is one of the most luxurious hotels in the world, known for its sail-like silhouette.

6. Go on a Desert Safari

A desert safari is a thrilling experience, offering dune bashing, camel rides, and traditional Bedouin entertainment.

7. Cruise Dubai Creek

Dubai Creek offers a glimpse into the city's history, with traditional abra boats and bustling souks along its banks.
